Individual Reflections:

(1) Jake

I have felt from the start that we have a great group and being 'Group 1', i.e. the first six people to jump in and start a group is a testament to our proactivity and enthusiasm. We communicate very effectively via our WhatsApp chat and have used this for our main form of communication rather than Teams. We work cohesively together and as time goes on it becomes more apparent where each members strengths lie and in what areas we can help each other more.

I have to say that I don’t think anybody was really prepared for how busy we all are in our personal lives and it has been hard to get everybody together to meet. I think now that we are more familiar with each other, we will be able to get started a bit earlier on our next group assignment and would probably benefit from scheduling regular meetings from the start running them for whoever could attend, rather than trying to postpone and reschedule.

Considering my nature, I was surprised by just how much I have enjoyed group work and working with new people and I feel really fortunate to be a part of such a proactive and friendly group. Through this group assignment I have learned just how very hard it is to organise six people to meet considering everybody’s different work, family, life and study commitments.

(3) Sneha

One thing that really surprised me while working with the members of the team is how well we work together. Each member of the team is so easy-going, considerate and willing to lend a helping hand where another team member is not able to complete a certain task. Given that the mode of study is solely online, I was concerned about being able to interact with other students and being able to gain exposure as you would with on campus learning. But I must be honest, I was truly impressed with how quickly each member got comfortable with each other. From the very beginning, we created a WhatsApp chat group. Doing this would mean that everyone would know the tasks they needed to complete within a certain timeframe. Overall, this project has taught me that teams can operate effectively regardless of individuals being in different locations and time zones. Our team was a prime example of this given that we have members from Adelaide, Melbourne, Sydney, Perth.

(7) Group Reflection

The group on the whole have reported an overwhelmingly positive experience for the assignment 2 collaboration. We have provided some very constructive feedback highlighting some core strengths of the group as well as some areas in which we can improve on for our next assignment together.

It is safe to say that each member has found it incredibly rewarding to be a part of this group and have really appreciated and fed off of each others positivity, enthusiasm and hard work. For those of us that have been in similar group environments for work or previous education purposes, we have been pleasantly surprised by just how cohesively we work together compared to prior experiences.

From the start we have been expert communicators and have relished the opportunity to get to know each other better personally and identify each other’s working skillset. We are considerate and empathetic towards each other and take into account everybody’s busy life and work commitments, familial emergencies and time off for holiday’s. We readily pick up where someone has had to drop off and use our full range of skills and capabilities to create a harmonious and productive whole.

Over the weeks, we developed some great techniques and tools that aided us greatly in our collaboration including our use of MS Teams to share our individual work and files. There we created a working To-Do-List where we assigned tasks to members with tentative completion dates. When we implemented this us productivity skyrocketed. We agree that by implementing this list early on in our next assignment we will be even more successful as a team.

We also feel that we underestimated the amount of time and work involved in getting our report converted to a website and ironing out all the issues involved with our html and css code and getting the website live on github pages. We agree that we will save ourselves a lot of stress by setting up a repository early on next time and building our website in a way that we can see the progress as it is made on GitHub Pages.

We recognise that we didn’t have many formal meetings on teams and this is due to our WhatsApp group conversation being so effective. We kept constantly up to date with current and upcoming tasks, personal issues that impacted the group, and provided feedback for each other’s work via WhatsApp.

While we held a couple of formal meetings, we agree that next time we should schedule regular meetings and stick to them, regardless of who can attend at the time. We will also hone our meeting skills including chairing, setting agendas and minute keeping.
